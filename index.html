Our Story

Gondo Skills Institute Security Services

Gondo Skills Institute Security Services is a South African company that provides security guards and security services to businesses and individuals. The company offers a wide range of security services, including armed response, unarmed response, CCTV monitoring, and access control. Gondo Skills Institute Security Services is a registered security company with the Private Security Industry Regulatory Authority (PSIRA).

Founded

Mpho Kumalo

Co-Founding Partner

Mpho Kumalo is a security professional with over 20 years of experience in the industry. He is the Co-Founder and Managing Director of Gondo Skills Institute Security Services, a leading provider of security training and consultancy services in South Africa.

Mpho is a qualified Security Risk Manager and has a Master of Business Administration degree from the University of Cape Town. He is also a member of the South African Institute of Security Professionals and the International Association of Chiefs of Police.
