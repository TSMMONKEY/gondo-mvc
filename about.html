At Gondo Skills Institute Security Services, we are a dedicated team of professionals committed to providing top-notch security solutions for our clients. With years of experience and a deep understanding of the security industry, we offer a comprehensive range of services designed to meet the unique needs of businesses and individuals. Our team consists of highly trained security personnel who possess the expertise and skills necessary to ensure the safety and protection of our clients. We take pride in our commitment to excellence and strive to deliver exceptional security services that exceed expectations.

A Proudly South African BBBEE owned company. Established in 2009, Gondo Skills Institute security is a reputable, security training academy. We have a proven clean track record which we have acquired over 14 years in the industry. Our over a million graduates are PSIRA accredited. The Directors combined security experience of 55 years coupled with our PSIRA accredited and SASSETA qualified assessors and moderators we ensure that our instructors are qualified to provide the required standard of specialised training in the following areas of security:

  • GradeEtoA
  • Cash-in-transit(CIT)
  • Satellite tracking
  • Forensics
  • Privateinvestigation

The mission of Gondo Skills Institute Security Services is to deliver quality training, provide a highly skilled workforce, and offer value-added security services to the public and private sectors

Our Mission

At Gondo Skills Institute Security Services, our mission is to provide unparalleled security solutions to our clients, ensuring their safety and peace of mind. We are driven by a commitment to excellence, professionalism, and integrity in all aspects of our work.

We understand that security is a fundamental need for businesses and individuals alike. Our mission is to create a safe and secure environment that allows our clients to focus on their core activities without worrying about potential threats or vulnerabilities.

To achieve this mission, we employ a highly trained team of security professionals who possess the expertise and skills necessary to handle diverse security challenges. We continuously invest in their training and development, ensuring they stay abreast of the latest industry trends, technological advancements, and best practices in security management.

Through a collaborative approach, we work closely with our clients to understand their specific security requirements, concerns, and goals. This enables us to tailor our services and solutions to meet their unique needs effectively. Whether it's implementing state-of-the-art surveillance systems, conducting comprehensive security assessments, or developing emergency response plans, we strive to deliver customized strategies that mitigate risks and enhance security.
